# Tariff Game

An interactive browser-based game for teaching game theory and trade policy.

## How it works
- Choose a trading partner
- Play 10 rounds
- Choose Low or High tariffs
- See how strategies affect outcomes

## Partners
- Stochastic: random behavior
- Cooperative: responds to your past move
- Dr. Winner: always high tariffs
